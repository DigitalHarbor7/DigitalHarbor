1Man1Band

Joshua Payne is a One-Man-Band from Cedar Rapids, Iowa, and takes his years of performance experience from all genres to create a sound like no other. Imagine hearing multiple voices and instruments and seeing only one man on stage creating it all right before your eyes. Josh has performed on stages all over the world from big to small, creating music in any capacity he could.
